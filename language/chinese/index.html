History

A combination of multiple spoken dialects, Chinese is one of the most spoken languages in the world, with around 17% of the population speaking a dialect of Chinese as their first language.

Modern Chinese is currently seperated into two different writing styles, with Simplified Chinese growing more popular in Mainland China, whilst Traditional Chinese staying as the common writing style in all other countries.

Job Overview

We look for quality and creativity.

Valuing creativity, we look for interpreters who not only interpret what's coming in, but communicate the meaning expertly going out.

Valuing quality, though we usually hire experienced veteran interpreters, we also bank on potential young talent, so don't be afraid to apply!

Job Pay

Our rates are extremely competitive. We have a minimum price per word, depending on language pair.

Job Requirements

Expert proficiency in English, Mandarin, Spanish, or Japanese.
Expert proficiency in a second language.
5 years experience as a working interpreter (highly recommended) or court certified interpreter

Job Process

Please email a resume and a sample translation of a creative work (poem, religious passage, literary works) to abacustranslation@gmail.com.
Please include [APPLICATION] [LANGUAGE 1/LANGUAGE 2] - [YOUR NAME] in the header. Ex: [APPLICATION] ENGLISH/SPANISH - JOHN SMITH.

Due to the high number of inquiries, we may not be able to respond to all applications.

You may then be prompted to take a short written exam, then a phone screen, then an interview.

If all goes well, welcome to the team!
