Our Services

Our translation services include interpreting full length novels and novellas.

Though the vast majority of our works are translations of books with the backing of the Big Four, we are open to any books published by promising indie publishers and self published authors wishing to share their story internationally.

Our Process

Send us an email regarding the book including a professional query letter, the first 5 pages, and any previous sales data or special circumstances.

We will provide you a quote within 12 hours.

For Indie/Self Publishers:


A flat fee will be charged for a majority of these works. However, a combination of royalty/flat fee may be negotiated, depending on quality of writing and marketability.
Upon acceptance, please pay a 10% fee upfront (if quote amount is over $10k) and send us the full, complete manuscript.
Please make sure your work is edited beforehand, as there may be additional charges for editing fees of the original work.


For Traditional Publishers:


A full flat fee or a royalty fee may be negotiated.
Upon acceptance, please send us the full manuscript.

Please contact us about potential long term partnerships.

Turnaround

For novels consisting of 100k words, expedited delivery takes around a month, with regular delivery being 2-3 months.

After completion, we will send you the completed work as well as an invoice for the remaining amount of services rendered. Annotations and specific explanations for using the words or phrases that we did, as well as background research and critiques of the novel, may also be provided upon (paid) request.

Pricing

Royalty options for established authors and prospective bestsellers.

Flat fee for indie/self publishers.

Though minor grammar, spelling, and sentence structure mistakes are allowed, major errors will be corrected for an additional editing fee.

Our Guarantee

Our service guarantees the quality of the translation. Our translation service leaves the writing, story, and/or overall plot unchanged. We are willing to sign an NDA to protect your work.

After completion, in the unlikely event in which you are not satisfied with the way the translated novel turned out, please send said parts back for further revision at no additional cost.
